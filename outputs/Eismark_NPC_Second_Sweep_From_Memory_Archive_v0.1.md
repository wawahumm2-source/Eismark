## Kaltheim

### Lord Protector Gravk Vagrkheim

Status: Strong canon candidate / major Kaltheim NPC

Role: Orc Lord Protector of the Sacrament; secular and military head of Kaltheim.

Recovered Details:
- Titles: Lord Protector of the Sacrament, Marshal of the Northern Host, Defender of the Pilgrimage.
- A legendary war hero and master tactician who rose through frontier campaigns.
- Credited with defeating major incursions, reforming Kaltheimian military doctrine, and modernizing parts of the armed forces.
- Brutish, direct, uncompromising, strategically sharp, and politically capable.
- Soldiers adore him; enemies fear him.
- Archive note: he fought as a young officer during the Great Continental War, so his age needs timeline confirmation.

Campaign Hook: The soldier-statesman of Kaltheim: blunt enough to feel dangerous, wise enough to keep the Sacrament alive.

### Grand Inquisitor Cassian Voss

Status: Strong canon candidate / major Kaltheim NPC

Role: Human Grand Inquisitor of the Inquisition of the Vigil.

Recovered Details:
- Titles: Grand Inquisitor, The Black Shepherd, Keeper of the Vigil, Warden of the Veins.
- Born in a northern Ashlands frontier village that vanished when he was twelve; only Cassian survived.
- Investigated Blood outbreaks, aberrant incursions, heretical cults, ancient ruins, and phenomena officially denied by the state.
- One of the few living people said to have stood before an active Vein.
- Gentle, patient, compassionate, observant, and terrifying when facing existential threats.
- Privately believes Uriel is still alive.
- Close friend of Archon Quintus Michael; their dynamic is described as The Sword and the Candle.

Campaign Hook: A compassionate hunter who proves Kaltheim can be merciful and frightening at the same time.

## Veloria

### President Theodore Ashcroft

Status: Strong canon candidate / major Velorian NPC

Role: Human President of the Commonwealth of Veloria; Valorian Federalist reformer.

Recovered Details:
- Titles: President of the Commonwealth of Veloria; Commander-in-Chief of the Commonwealth Armed Forces.
- Born to a middle-class family in Veleris.
- Served as an officer in the Commonwealth Army, then as a lawyer and Senator.
- Rose to the Presidency on promises to fight corruption, strengthen infrastructure, expand opportunity, and preserve democratic institutions.
- Charismatic, sincere, deeply principled, and fundamentally decent.
- Marcus Vale respects him but believes he is too idealistic; OTS may act without his knowledge.

Campaign Hook: The honest reformer trying to govern a republic full of corruption, spies, prohibition failures, and impossible threats.

### Director Grizzik Bramblegear

Status: Strong canon candidate / major Velorian NPC

Role: Goblin Director of the Commonwealth Arcane Commission.

Recovered Details:
- Titles: Director of the CAC, Professor Emeritus of Hartfoot Magic Institute, Senior Fellow of the Commonwealth Geographical Society.
- Born in the industrial districts of Veleris; underestimated by nearly everyone.
- Graduated from Hartfoot Magic Institute with highest honors.
- Renowned scholar of magical law, respected abjuration specialist, and expert on magical safety.
- Professional, disciplined, uncompromising, and dreaded by illegal mages.
- Deeply distrusts the OTS, considering them too willing to ignore law in pursuit of results.
- Secretly studied Blood artifacts, Second Age relics, and aberrant phenomena; the knowledge terrifies him.

Campaign Hook: The magical safety bureaucrat who may be one of the most dangerous responsible people in Veloria.
