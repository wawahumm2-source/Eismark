## Review Categories

- Already Canon: already incorporated into the ECD Archive or readable guide.
- Should be Promoted: strong candidate material not yet present in canon.
- Needs Revision: good concept, but outdated, incomplete, duplicated, or changed by later lore.
- Archived / Superseded: preserved idea replaced by newer canon or another backlog item.
- Contradicts Canon: conflicts with current ECD and should remain non-canon unless explicitly retconned.
- Do Not Promote: large incompatibility or obsolete frame that should remain source-history only.

## Major Incompatibilities / Do Not Promote

These recovered geopolitics elements are useful as development history but should not be added to canon:

1. "The Waking Republic" is obsolete. Use NAT-002 - The Commonwealth of Veloria and GOV-002 - Government of the Commonwealth of Veloria.
2. Any recovered Velorian government draft is superseded by the modern ECD structure: constitutional presidential republic, Assembly of Commons, Senate, High Court, and the Office of Tactical Security's unusual oversight problem.
3. Exact regional placements from early geopolitics drafts are provisional until the map audit is complete. Names such as the Iron Coast, Ashlands, Sylvan Veil, and Gn'ure can be used, but borders and neighboring relationships should not be locked from older text.
4. Alternative regional frameworks such as "Crownless Kingdoms," "Grey Frontier," or early secondary-power lists should not replace GEO-001 through GEO-014 unless explicitly reworked and approved.
5. The Ashlands material is compatible only in revised form: Southern Ashlands as frontier/mining/scavenger territory, Northern Ashlands as ruin/Blood/relic expedition country, and the region as Eismark's archaeological frontier.
