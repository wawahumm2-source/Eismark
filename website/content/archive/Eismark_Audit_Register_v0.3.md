### CAP-014 - Holy Machine Taxonomy

Status: Resolved

Final Canon:

- Automatons: closest equivalent to Warforged in standard D&D; mechanical constructs built through conventional artificing.
- Consecrated: Eismark's unique evolution of the Warforged concept; living sacred constructs with distinct lore, appearance, origins, and spiritual significance.
- Exalted Engines: medium-to-large mechanical counterparts of the Consecrated; conscious towering war machines rather than humanoid constructs.
- Joivians: ancient holy war machines of Ancient Kaltheim, built around dormant vessels of lesser angels, with Uriel's Joivian as the sole First One exception.

Lineage:

- Consecrated descend philosophically and technologically from later attempts to recreate the Joivians.
- Exalted Engines are the larger-scale evolution of the Consecrated.
- Automatons are a separate technological lineage.
