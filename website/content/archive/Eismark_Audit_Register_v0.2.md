## CAP - Canon Inconsistencies

### CAP-001 - HIS-005 Numbering Conflict

Status: Resolved.

Conflict: `Eismark_Canon_Database_History_001-005.pdf` uses HIS-005 for "The Mortal Races Are Created," while `ECD_Adjusted_History_Timeline_v1.pdf` uses HIS-005 for "The First Age Begins."

Final Canon: HIS-005 is "The First Age Begins."

Resolution: The previous assignment, "HIS-005 - The Mortal Races Are Created," is deprecated and should be renumbered during the next ECD history audit.

### CAP-002 - First Ones Count and Angel Taxonomy

Status: Resolved.

Conflict: Some sources describe roughly fifteen First Ones, while Vardheim histories use "Angels" broadly and say countless Angels walked Vardheim, with six foremost.

Final Canon: "Angel" is the broad term for divine celestial beings created by the Creator. Angels are divided into First Ones and Lesser Angels.

Resolution:

- First Ones are also known as Archangels or High Angels.
- First Ones are the highest order of angels.
- First Ones were created directly by the Creator and entrusted with shaping Creation.
- First Ones create the lesser angels.
- Lesser Angels are divine servants and caretakers of Creation, subordinate to the First Ones.
- "Angels walked Vardheim" may refer broadly to both First Ones and Lesser Angels.

### CAP-003 - Created First Ones vs Sent First Ones

Status: Resolved.

Conflict: ECD/timeline sources say the Creator creates the First Ones; Vardheim history says the First Ones were sent into Vardheim.

Final Canon:

1. The Creator creates the First Ones.
2. The Creator creates Creation.
3. The Creator sends the First Ones into Vardheim to shape it.

Resolution: Both source traditions are true when placed in sequence.

### CAP-004 - Cause of the Veins Opening

Status: Resolved.

Conflict: Some sources say the Entity discovers Creation; Vardheim histories say Kaltheim scholars discovered the multiverse and attracted the Entity's attention.

Final Canon: The Entity became aware of Creation through the actions of mortal scholars.

Resolution: The scholars did not summon the Entity. Their discoveries merely caused Creation to become visible to it.

### CAP-005 - Communion vs World-Sealing Miracle

Status: Resolved.

Conflict: ECD/Uriel sources say the Communion seals the Veins; Vardheim histories describe the Angels pouring essence into Vardheim itself without clearly naming the Communion.

Final Canon: The First Ones' sacrifice and the creation of the Communion are the same event.

Resolution:

1. First Ones sacrifice themselves.
2. The Communion forms.
3. Reality is sealed.

### CAP-006 - Joivian Construction

Status: Needs ruling.

Conflict: Later timeline sources say Joivians are usually built around lesser angels; Vardheim history says each Jovian was built around a First One.

Required Action: Define the final Joivian construction rule.

### CAP-007 - Joivian/Jovian Spelling

Status: Resolved.

Conflict: ECD/timeline/Uriel sources use `Joivian`; Vardheim histories use `Jovian`.

Final Canon: `Joivian`.

Resolution: `Jovian` is a deprecated spelling.

### CAP-008 - Did All Joivians March Into the Great Maw?

Status: Needs wording clarification.

Conflict: Timeline says the Joivians marched into the Great Maw and never returned; Uriel canon says the Uriel-linked Joivian stayed behind as The Abandoned One.

Required Action: Update timeline wording to account for the exception, or decide if Uriel canon supersedes the broad timeline phrasing.

### CAP-009 - Great Forgetting Timing

Status: Needs timeline clarification.

Conflict: Uriel's departure causes the Great Forgetting, but sources differ on whether it is immediate, a later cultural period, or both.

Required Action: Define cause vs historical manifestation.

### CAP-010 - Ancient Kaltheim Location

Status: Needs geography ruling.

Conflict: Vardheim history places ancient Kaltheim beneath northern mountains; timeline says modern Kaltheim is founded in the northern mountains after pilgrimage and Ancient Kaltheim's ruins later give rise to Veloria.

Required Action: Clarify Ancient Kaltheim, modern Kaltheim, and Veloria's geographic relationship.

### CAP-011 - Kaltheim First City vs Modern Kaltheim

Status: Naming ambiguity.

Conflict: "Kaltheim" refers to the ancient first city, later fortress civilization, and modern mountain theocracy without always distinguishing them.

Required Action: Define naming conventions: Ancient Kaltheim, modern Kaltheim, Kaltheim city/kingdom/theocracy.

### CAP-012 - Blood Ceased Flowing vs Blood Returning

Status: Wording tension.

Conflict: Vardheim history says the Blood ceased flowing after the Veins were sealed, but also says Blood remained; modern sources suggest the Blood may be returning.

Required Action: Clarify whether new Blood stopped entering while old Blood remained.
